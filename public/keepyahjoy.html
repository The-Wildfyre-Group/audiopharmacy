Keepyajoy

Keep yah joy is the next Hype Williams. The CEO of KYJ productions is the most proactive video director in the San Francisco-Bay Area today. Born and raised in Long Island, this New Yorker came to Cali ready to work. With the personal goal of shooting one video per week, Keep has set the tone that starts when he wakes up and ends when he goes to sleep. Keep is a workaholic. He is the resin that completes the harmonious experience called Audiopharmacy.
