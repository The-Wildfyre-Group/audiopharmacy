Pasha Brown

Pasha Brown has recently completed his 808 training from the 707 to the 408 and got the 411 on it. Born in Southern California and raised in the Bay Area, Pasha Brown is not a product of his environment. Pasha Brown completed and released "Cadillacs and Cockroaches" in 2004 and helped in the production of the following 5 years of Audiopharmacy releases. Such as his production on the Album "Spare Change - the Wickedtree."

"He adds that extra HIP HOP flavor to the group, but doesn't compromise the organic flow neither." Keep explained to the managers of the American Music Abroad.

Pasha Brown is currently working on a project with the Audiopharmacy collective. It will be available in April 20th 2015.
